Side B

Routine, Disrupted — Before Uni vs. University

Moving from home life into university broke both listeners' routines. Research on listening behavior finds that when a personal routine is disrupted, people don't retreat to old favorites — they explore more (Kim et al., PNAS 2024). The four panels below ask: how much did each person listen, when did they listen, did the routine break come with a burst of exploration, and how much did each habit shift overall?
